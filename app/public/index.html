Oconee County Fire Rescue


Our Mission:

Here at the Oconee County Fire Rescue when asked if a fire needs to be put out our response is "it depends". We are a fire consulting service that can meet all of your fire needs. Whether it be the safe dismantling of an existing fire system, developing a fire strategy or identifying risk and mitigations for new fires. Through our established frameworks we are allowed to tackle fires from all possible angles. We are able to guarantee a 99% success rate when putting out fires with our patented 13 slide deck.
